About mealmate

Do you want to meet new people, but are shy or aren't sure how? Do you barely have time to eat with the people you know, or do you usually eat alone? If you answered yes to either of these questions, then Website Name can help you resolve both of those things! Input your availability, interests, and preferred dining halls, and we'll suggest some other students with whom you could eat.
