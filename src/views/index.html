WHAT IS A mealmate?

(noun)

a friend (or peer that you will befriend) who eats a meal with you at a MIT dining hall

STEPS TO FIND YOUR mealmate

1. Log in through Facebook!

2. Edit your profile! Your matches can see your profile.

3. Fill out your availability for a given day, then click "Find my mealmate!"

4. Wait for a confirmation email saying that you've been matched to someone.

5. Check your matches page for your match!

6. Eat with your mealmate and have a great time!
