INSPIRATION FOR mealmate


While taking 6.148, MIT's Web Development Class and Competition, Cynthia, Nanxi, and Sabrina really wanted to solve problems and help people at MIT. Some problems that we saw through personal experience, talking to others, and reading online sources (ex: MIT Confessions and MIT Timely Confessions) were that some MIT students are lonely, shy, have trouble making friends, greenbox every meal, or eat alone in dining halls.

We wanted to make this website to help MIT students meet and befriend other MIT students and brighten their lives. We hope beautiful friendships will form and MIT students will be happier from this.
