INSPIRATION FOR mealmate


While taking 6.148, MIT's Web Development Class and Competition, Cynthia, Nanxi, and Sabrina really wanted to solve problems and help people at MIT. We saw through personal experience, talking to others, and reading online sources (ex: MIT Confessions and MIT Timely Confessions) that some MIT students are lonely, shy, have trouble making friends, greenbox every meal, or eat alone in dining halls.

We developed mealmate to help MIT students meet and befriend peers and to brighten their lives. We hope beautiful friendships will form and MIT students will be happier from sharing delicious meals with good company.
